About this home

Rare opportunity to own a private, gated estate at the end of a quiet road on a level 2.15-acre lot in Kenmore! Enjoy peace, privacy, and park-like grounds with scenic views. This light-filled home features vaulted ceilings, skylights, and bay windows. All brand new: roof, flooring, carpet, interior paint, kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities. The main level offers a bright kitchen and living area that opens to a large deck. The oversized primary suite includes a 5-piece bath, walk-in closet, and French doors. Car and boat enthusiasts will appreciate the 6 garages, RV garage, shop and storage. New Kenmore city Building code allows this huge land to build 2 ADUs, buyers to verify. Plus, no HOA dues.
